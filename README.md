# cheniflora
🌸 Cheniflora

Flowers That Never Fade, Memories That Never Leave

Cheniflora is an AI-powered handcrafted flower gifting platform designed to transform emotional gifting through personalized chenille flower bouquets. Unlike traditional flowers that wilt over time, Cheniflora offers forever blooms that preserve memories and emotions while supporting handmade artisans.

---

🚀 Problem Statement

Traditional flowers have a limited lifespan and often lack personalization. Handmade creators face challenges in reaching customers and showcasing their creativity online.

How can we create a digital platform that enables artisans to sell handcrafted floral products while helping customers discover meaningful, long-lasting gifts?

---

💡 Solution

Cheniflora bridges the gap between emotional gifting and artisan craftsmanship by providing:

- 🌷 Handmade forever flowers
- 🎁 Personalized gifting experiences
- 🤖 AI-powered bouquet recommendations
- ✍️ AI-generated gift messages
- 🎨 Custom bouquet builder
- 📱 Mobile-first shopping experience

---
